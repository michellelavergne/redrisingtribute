A time line of important events before the start of Red Rising

  • 0 PCE -- Silenius au Lune conquers Earth, founding The Society.
  • Mid-2nd Century PCE -- The Terraforming of Io beings.
  • 3rd Century PCE -- The Obsidians instigate the Dark Revolt against the Golds, results in the massacre of 90% of their race.
  • Early 5th Century PCE -- The Colors not already working in the Helium mines begin their migration to Mars.
  • 493 PCE -- The first docks of Ganymede shipyards are completed
  • 630s PCE -- Octavia Au Lune, Lorne au Arcos and the Ash Lord are born.
  • 640s PCE -- Nine year old Octavia au Lune incites a riot by throwing diamonds from an aircar.
  • 649 PCE -- Nero au Augustus is born.
  • 656 PCE -- The beginning of the House War between House Augustus and House Bellona.
  • 670 PCE -- Lorne au Arcos becomes the Rage Knight.
  • 696 PCE -- Dancer is born.
  • 707 PCE -- Ephraim ti Horn is born.
  • 720 PCE -- Darrow and Sevro au Barca are born.
  • 721 PCE -- Virginia and Adrius au Augustus are born.
  • 733 PCE -- Darrow begins working in the mines of Lykos.
  • 736 PCE -- Darrow and Eo marry and the events of Red Rising begin.
